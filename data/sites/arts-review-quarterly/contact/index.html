Pitch Guidelines

If you're interested in writing for Arts Review Quarterly, please include:

  • A brief pitch (2-3 paragraphs) describing your proposed review or essay
  • Why this subject matters now
  • Your qualifications and relevant writing samples
  • Proposed word count and deadline

We aim to respond to all pitches within two weeks, though response times may vary during busy periods.
