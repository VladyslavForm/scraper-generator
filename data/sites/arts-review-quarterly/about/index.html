About Arts Review Quarterly

Arts Review Quarterly is an independent publication dedicated to thoughtful, in-depth criticism of contemporary arts and culture. Founded in 2020, we believe that cultural criticism matters now more than ever‚Äînot as gatekeeping, but as conversation, as a way to understand ourselves and our world through the art we create and consume.

Our Mission

We provide comprehensive reviews and essays across Film, Music, and Books, guided by the belief that great criticism should be as engaging as the art it examines. Our writers bring expertise, passion, and diverse perspectives to every review, aiming to illuminate rather than simply judge.

Our Values

Independence: We accept no advertising and maintain complete editorial independence. Our reviews are based solely on artistic merit and critical analysis.

Depth: We take the time to engage deeply with works, providing context, analysis, and thoughtful critique rather than quick hot takes.

Diversity: We're committed to reviewing work from diverse creators and ensuring our critics bring varied perspectives and experiences to their analysis.

Accessibility: We write for passionate audiences, not insiders. Our criticism is rigorous but never needlessly obscure.

Our Team

Our editorial team includes Sarah Mitchell and David Chen (Film), Marcus Reynolds and Alicia Thompson (Music), and Robert Williams and Jennifer Park (Books). Together, we bring decades of combined experience in cultural criticism and a genuine love for the arts we cover.
